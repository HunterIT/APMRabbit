WELCOME TO THE RABBIT EYE

 

Welcome,
The following application was created by Students at Humber College. This application is an open-source project under the GPLv3 license. The application is used on a GoPro camera with WiFi compatibly. The website is located on the SD-Card of the GoPro. It has access to

  • Stream the GoPro Camera
  • View Photos & Videos
  • Take Photos & Videos
  • Change GoPro Settings
